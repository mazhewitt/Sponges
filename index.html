Jacqu Snapp is a Bahamian sponge exporting company that has its own sponge fishing boat.Consequently we can offer our sponges at a more competitive rate than the Nassau based exporters. Our sponges are cut from the sea bed leaving behind enough live sponge at each cut to ensure rapid regeneration. thus preserving the sustainability of the sponge beds. We can supply sponges for bathing, spas, pottery, decorating, bathroom ornaments, equine hygiene, artwork and luxury vehicle cleaning. It is our preference to sell our sponges unbleached as bleaching can cause them to be more fragile, reducing their lifespan. In their natural state the sponges are a light brown colour, not bright yellow.
